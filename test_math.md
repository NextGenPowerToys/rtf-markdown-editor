# Math Support Testing

## Inline Math

The quadratic formula is $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$.

## Display Math

$$E = mc^2$$

$$\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i}{n}$$

## Multiple Formulas

The mean is $\mu = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i$ and the variance is $\sigma^2 = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n} (x_i - \mu)^2$.

## Complex Formula

$$\int_0^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}$$
